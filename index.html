How To Buy

STEP 1: Get Ethereum (ETH)

Buy ETH from a centralized exchange (Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, etc.) or have it in your crypto wallet (like MetaMask).

STEP 2: Connect Your Wallet

Open a DeFi platform that supports $UPTOBER (e.g., Uniswap, Curve, or Abracadabra). Connect your wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.).

STEP 3: Swap ETH for $UPTOBER

- Choose ETH as the token you want to trade.
- Select $UPTOBER as the token you want to receive.
- Enter the amount and confirm the transaction.

STEP 4: Confirm & Store Safely

Once the swap is complete, $UPTOBER will appear in your wallet. Make sure you add the official contract address to track your balance correctly.
